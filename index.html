The Matrix (1999)

Directed by: The Wachowskis

Summary

AI dominates a dystopian future, and a hacker named Neo discovers reality is a simulation called the Matrix.

Why I Love It

This movie blew my mind. The blend of philosophy, sci-fi, and special effects was revolutionary in 1999.
